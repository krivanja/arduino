PUSH BUTTON

This project is helps in understanding the internal connections in a Push Button. Arduino is just a power source.

Hardware Required

  • 1X Arduino
  • 1X Breadboard
  • 2X LED
  • 2X Resistor (220 Ω)
  • Jumper Wires

Circuit

Connect the center pin of a potentiometer to pin A0, and the outside pins to +5V and ground.
