SERIES CIRCUIT

This project is taken from the book "Arduino Projects Book" of the Starter Kit. The two switches are in series. This means that the same electrical current flows through both of them, so that they both have to be pressed for the LED to light up. Arduino is just the power source for this circuit

Hardware Required

  • Arduino UNO
  • Breadboard
  • Push Button
  • LED
  • Resistor (220 Ω)
  • Jumper wires

Circuit

Connect the center pin of a potentiometer to pin A0, and the outside pins to +5V and ground.
