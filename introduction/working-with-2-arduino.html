WORKING WITH TWO ARDUINO

This project is for illustration purposes only. This may not have any practical application in real world. It will help beginners in understanding about the working of Arduino Pins

Hardware Required

  • 2X Arduino
  • 2X Breadboard
  • 2X LED
  • 2X Resistor (220 Ω)
  • Jumper Wires

Circuit

Connect the center pin of a potentiometer to pin A0, and the outside pins to +5V and ground.
