DIGITAL READ SERIAL

This example demonstrates how to read a digital input on pin 2, prints the result to the Serial Monitor.

Hardware Required

  • Arduino UNO
  • Breadboard
  • Push Button
  • Resistor (220 Ω)
  • Jumper wires

Circuit

Connect the center pin of a potentiometer to pin A0, and the outside pins to +5V and ground.
