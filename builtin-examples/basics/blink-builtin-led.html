BLINK (BUILTIN LED)

Most Arduinos have an on-board LED you can control. This LED is connected to a digital pin and its number may vary from board type to board type. On the UNO, it is attached to digital pin 13. The constant LED_BUILTIN is set to the correct LED pin independent of which board is used and allows you to control the built-in LED easily.

Hardware Required

  • Arduino UNO
